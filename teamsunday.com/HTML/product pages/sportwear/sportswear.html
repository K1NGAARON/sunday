Recycled Sportswear

Finding sportswear that is ecologically sustainable and has a cool design can be hard. We, at Sunday, want to be a sustainable partner for our clients.

That’s why we put a lot of effort into R&D to find the perfect materials that are also sustainable. We can now offer a recycled sportswear collection that can be perfectly personalized in your corporate branding. Transparency is crucial in this process, so every recycled sports jersey gets a clear hang tag stating which materials were used in its production.
