1. Recruitment at job fairs

    2. With the slogan: “Een job die je van je sokken blaast.”, they want to trigger students to have a conversation with their recruiters. By adding the possibility to participate in a give-away of the socks, they stand out as a company. Together with the socks, information on future job opportunities were sent to a number of winners.

  2. Online recruitment

    3. Next to students, Distrilog wants to attract experienced warehouse workers, drivers, administrative assistants, … . If someone gets hired, they receive a pair of sports socks as part of their onboarding gift package.

  3. Internal give-away

    4. Distrilog wants to organize an internal give-away on social media for its own employees. They choose a winner out of the employees that post something nice about work or share a photo/video on their personal socials each month.
