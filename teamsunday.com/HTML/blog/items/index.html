Welcome gift ideas for new employee [5 Examples]

Welcome gifts for new employees are the equivalent of taking fresh cookies to your new neighbors. They help you establish a positive rapport and demonstrate a friendly (work) environment.

A welcome package conveys a very clear message to new employees even if it is only a minor part of a much bigger onboarding procedure. You are making an effort to show your hires how glad you are that they are joining your team.

Since the list of branded swag is endless, here are our five favorite welcome gift ideas for your upcoming new employee onboarding box.

1. Branded socks

What better way to celebrate a new job than with branded socks? Cater the basic needs of your employees, because let’s be honest we all need socks.

2. Caps or beanies

Be head-on with your gifts! Consider modest baseball caps with a rubber patch or embroidered snapbacks.

3. Matching T-shirt & Hoodie

Go for more than just a logo on a plain white t-shirt! Create unique t-shirts, hoodies & make sure your people will love to wear them.

4. Not to be missed

Who has never received a reusable bottle or a branded notebook and pen? Those are the classics.They just work, they always have.

5. Big bags

Bag it up! Bags are a stylish option for the present day because they come in a broad variety of forms, sizes, styles, fabrics, and vibrant designs.

Conclusion

Many people experience both apprehension and enthusiasm when starting a new job. A welcome gift helps calm some of the nerves that come with starting somewhere new. By providing something exciting next to long training sessions, helps to make the onboarding process less intimidating.
