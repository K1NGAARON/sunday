5 items for a perfect Onboarding Box

Having branded company swag is the best way to give employees the impression that they have been hired officially. It's a great method to make new employees feel like they belong to the team as well as to increase the visibility of your company in front of more potential employees and customers.

Your new employees will never forget their perfect onboarding box.

How do you figure out what swag should be included in your onboarding box for new employees? A simple way to start creating this welcome packet is by asking yourself these simple questions:

  • How do you want to convey your company culture to this new employee?
  • What will this new hire need on a daily basis to do their job effectively?
  • How will you make this new hire feel welcome and want to stay?

Although the requirements of each company vary, there are some general elements that should be taken into account for any welcome packet. These 5 items are a must-have and should serve as a reference guide for the employee onboarding process.

1. Take notes

What’s more fun than an old-fashioned branded notebook, pen or coffee mug to go to your next meeting? Those are the classics.They just work, they always have. Think about these office supplies as a place to start for your onboarding collection.

Tip: Write some sweet words by the CEO on the first page.

2. Socks on-brand & on-trend

Your feet are important and socks are a universal garment. Everyone wears them, everyone can use them at any time and certainly within any job. Socks are a rewarding product when they are high quality. Imagine your brand represented perfectly on- brand, on-trend and made in a sustainable way!

3. Well hooded

By giving them a hoodie or other piece of clothing from your brand, you will be visible and tangible for a long period of time in their lives. On top of that, clothing caters to the basic needs of a human being, if that’s not a powerful gesture, we don’t know what is.

4. Size does matter

Tote bags are available in a wide range of shapes, sizes, styles, materials and thriving designs, making them a fashionable choice for the modern period. Tote bags are the greatest choice for anyone looking for an all-purpose bag that can easily switch from a day bag to a travel bag. And the bigger the bag, the bigger the branding can be.

5. Head banging headwear

Headwear products are the perfect products to create those unique collector's items. Think of embroidered snapbacks or subtle baseball caps with a rubber patch. There’s a broad range of decoration possibilities that perfectly match your brand, with the creative guidance of headwear designers.
