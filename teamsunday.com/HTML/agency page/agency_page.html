FAQ?

Why is building a community so important for your company?

The war for talent is huge. People value activities next to the actual job very highly. As a company, you have to stand out from all the rest.
Your current employees are your most important marketing tools. The way they speak about your company can make or break your image to the outside world. That is why building a strong connection between the company and its employees is so important.

Why is custom clothing the number 1 tool to boost employer branding

Building a strong connection between the company and its employees can be boosted with offline tools. Clothing is thé number 1 tool to do so. Why? Clothing is very personal. Literally nobody is wearing something that they do not support or stand behind.
Together with you, we make sure that your client receives the most awesome design, 100% on brand, combined with the best quality, sustainability and comfort. People proudly wearing your brand, even on Sunday, is the ultimate goal you want to achieve.

What does our experience tell us?

Companies receive the most fabulous rebrandings when cooperating with creative agencies. Only when it comes to customized clothing, these companies are left out in the dark and don't find the right supplier to deliver the newly branded clothing.
Besides crappy quality, branding is not on point and mostly very standard. This results in a disappointing feeling and a not 100% successful branding project.

What advantages do I have as an agency?

Offering this extra service to your customer will not only profile you as a full service agency, you will also generate extra revenue. We'll support you in creating company fashion for your client. After all your creative work has been done, we'll do all the operational hassle for you. From technical files to production to delivery. Next to the extra commission fee, samples will be provided during the process.

Why Sunday?

We are not an ordinary embroidery shop. We start from design on a blank page and produce unique clothing. This offers you endless possibilities to translate your creative work onto clothing. Next to design, sustainability, quality and service are very important in our daily business.
