Customize branded backpacks

We create backpacks from scratch that match your brand.

  • Unisex and unisize product
  • Premium quality
  • Sustainably produced in Europe

Perfect corporate gift

Young twenty-something or experienced fifty-something. Sporty style or rather classic. There is a suitable backpack for everyone. This accessory therefore lends itself perfectly as an ideal corporate gift.

Backpacks used to be seen as a handy accessory for sportspeople, but fortunately this bag has now proven its usefulness for multiple profiles such as students, interns and business people.

Get your free quote ➔

Inspiration

Ultimate design

For a backpack to be successful, 2 things are taken into account: the fitting, how does the backpack fit ergonomically and is it convenient to use? Plus the design, does the backpack look nice and can be easily combined with daily outfits.

Our R&D team invested a lot of time and effort in researching different fittings in order to come up with the perfect model: a backpack that is comfortable, fits nicely and has enough compartments to store items in a convenient and safe way.

With our backpacks, it is also possible to personalize them as desired and place them in the corporate branding. Ranging from patches to embroidery and all-over patterns, we like to think along with you what is most suitable for your business.

Sustainability is the way to go

We consider it important to work with sustainable materials (without compromising on quality). Our offer therefore includes 2 backpacks made from recycled PET bottles. A nice choice for companies that are also committed to a greener world.
