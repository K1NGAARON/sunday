6 Winter Gifts packages for Employees that Will Make Them Happy in the Holiday Season

With the soaring energy prices, it can be difficult to stay warm. No heating is the new standard in many offices.

Ensuring that your team is as warm as possible during winter is a human necessity. So, what are you waiting for?

Having a good pair of socks, a knitted sweater or a hat and scarf will warm your employees' and customers' hearts and will give them something to think about your brand all the time!

Here's our list of six great gift packages for your employees:

1. Socks, Beanies & Scarves

Socks, beanies and scarves form the perfect trio of winter accessories. You can both protect yourself from the cold and create unique combinations that reflect your brand’s identity.

2. Branded blankets

Let’s face it, when it’s snowing outside, all you really want to do is curl up in front of the fireplace with your loved ones. Add a blanket to the mix and the coziness is complete. Send around some blankets, write a message on the wrapper and let your employees know that a winter break is deserved. Truly a sign of care and appreciation.

3. Big bags

Bag it up! Bags are a stylish option for the present day because they come in a broad variety of forms, sizes, styles, fabrics, and vibrant designs. Your employees will love to use it when going on a winter break.

4. Matching jacket & hoodie

Those last rays of sunshine combined with some chilly wind call for a cool hoodie with a matching jacket. Hoodies are comfortable, warm and have a youthful look and feel to them. Together they form the ideal winter-prove outfit.

5. Custom Sportswear

Want your employees to keep their new year's resolutions longer than 2 days? Motivate them by providing them with company fitness clothing! And even better when your brand’s logo accompanies them!

6. Knitted jumper

The days of celebrating are upon us, and my oh my is everyone jolly! The ultimate winter swag is our knitted jumper, a 100% customizable wool sweater that’ll keep the freezing temperatures from getting to the holiday happiness. Your employees will be proud ambassadors at the family dinner table!

Conclusion

The first thing you should do now is get started! Winter really is coming. Let your company’s colors and logo be a part of the holiday cheer. Luckily most of these products are still available at Sunday. You'll find everything from socks and beanies to jackets and sportswear.
